Multiparameter, Portable, Color Screen Monitor CSI 8100
CSI8100 is a flexible, multiparameter, color screen, easy to use and portable non invasive bedside monitor. Having CO2 , CO2/O2 and IBP (2) as additional options, the CSI 8100 can be used in any ambient from Adult needs to Pediatric or Neonatal department as bedside monitor or in transit monitor. CSI mounts easily and quickly in walls, IV stands and bed rails.
Design as compact, lightweight and simple user interface, CSI 8100, allow to user to focus on patients, not to monitor, improving the patient management.
